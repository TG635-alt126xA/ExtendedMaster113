888	SLC 888***

Indovision / MNC Vision broadcasts on SES-7 satellite using 10 transponders. However, a number of channels are not accessible to subscribers for various reasons (i.e: channels no longer broadcast, for internal purposes, smartcard not compatible, channel slots for backup or trials, etc.). But all of those channels can be received using an FTA/generic set-top box.

*) Depending on the type of set-top box. Vision Prime channel in SDTV format broadcast on 2 different transponders, so it is possible to have duplicate channels on set-top boxes that still use SDTV/MPEG-4 format (i.e: JH-2000, SMT-S1080, SMT-S1081, etc.), or customers who have HD set-top box but don't take the HD package or not pay the HD set-top box rental fee.
**) Channels cannot be displayed on SDTV set-top box (audio only)
***) For internal use only (whitelist smartcard & paired set-top box)
